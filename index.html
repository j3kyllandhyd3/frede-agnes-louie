OUR MISSION

The JWC Foundation is a global coalition of charitable trusts united by a single purpose: to save the world. We direct an army of volunteers that operate worldwide to bring hope to desperate communities. Our campaigns are determined by our membership. In 2017, we are focused on child vaccination, refugee action bringing free education to deprived areas, and combatting climate change.
